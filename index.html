ABOUT

Introducing Dr. Stella Yoo

유교수님 소개

Stella Yoo, MD, was born in Seoul, South Korea, and immigrated to Koreatown, Los Angeles, at the age of three. She spent her childhood in various cities including Lincoln, Nebraska, for kindergarten and Dallas, Texas, for elementary school before moving back to Seoul from fourth grade to sixth grade. Her adolescent years in Seoul were pivotal in fostering bilingualism and biculturalism, which equip her to be a culturally sensitive physician for today’s Korean immigrant patients in Koreatown.

Her education and training are as follows:

  • High school at Oxford Academy, Orange County
  • Undergraduate education at University of California, Berkeley
  • Medical school education at Temple University in Philadelphia, PA
  • Internship in internal medicine at Abington Memorial Hospital
  • Residency in radiation oncology at University of Southern California (USC)

Her leadership roles include serving as a board member of the national organization of Korean American Society of Radiation Oncology (KASTRO, treasurer) and the local chapter of the Korean American Graduate Medical Association (KAGMA, vice president). Dr. Yoo’s academic projects include Korean cancer outreach programs such as survivorship care and educational seminars for the community. Her vision for the future is to raise awareness to the needs of the Korean people, who are otherwise considered a small ethnic minority in America.

Dr. Yoo의 교육 및 훈련은 다음과 같습니다.

  • 오렌지 카운티 옥스퍼드 아카데미 고등학교 졸업
  • UC Berkeley에서 대학부 교육이수
  • 펜실베이니아주 필라델피아에 있는 Temple University의 의과대학 교육이수
  • Abington Memorial Hospital에서 내과 인턴십 수료
  • University of Southern California(USC) 방사선종양학과 레지던트 수료

한미방사선종양학회 전국조직(KASTRO, 재무) 및 한미대학원의사회(KAGMA, 부회장) 지역지부 이사로 활동하고 있습니다. 현재 Dr. Yoo의 학술 프로젝트에는 ‘암 생존자 관리 프로그램’과 지역 사회를 위한 교육 세미나를 비롯한 ‘한국 암 아웃리치 프로그램’이 포함됩니다. 앞으로 Dr. Yoo의 비전은 미국에서 소수 민족으로 간주되는 한인 이민자들의 이슈에 대한 인식을 높이는 것입니다.
